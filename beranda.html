How to Use the Library Application

Follow these simple steps to get the most out of our library application:

  1. Register or Login: Create an account or log in to access personalized features.
  2. Search for Books: Use the search bar to find books by title, author, or category.
  3. Borrow Books: Select books from the catalog and request to borrow them.
  4. Manage Transactions: Check the status of your borrow requests and due dates in the "Transaksi" section.
  5. Update Your Profile: Keep your profile information up to date in the "Profil" section.
  6. Logout: Ensure your account is secure by logging out after use.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team.
